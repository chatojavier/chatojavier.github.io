About me

My name is Javier Benavides. I am a publicist by profession and a future fullstack developer. I'm currently studying the Fullstack development professional program at MITxPro. For many years of my life I worked in marketing departments, developing traditional and online marketing. 4 years ago I decided to become independent and provide marketing and consulting services to companies. So some of these companies started asking me to develop their websites. At first I started only with the user experience and the interface part, but step by step I went deeper into the world of development and I was fascinated. Thus, during the pandemic, I began to study basic languages ​​myself such as HTML, CSS (which I am quite fluent in now), Javascript and a bit of PHP (the latter because I started creating my own themes in Wordpress). While I learned a lot, I found that I needed to enroll in a more rigorous course to advance programming. Thus I began to study the aforementioned professional program.

Thanks to what I have learned, I have managed to integrate APIs into my developments, as well as manipulate JSON information and create dynamic websites.

Today I dedicate 80% of my work time to web design and development, which I love, and I hope in the future to improve my skills in a technology company that give me the opportunity.
